Video games are often viewed as a simple form of entertainment, but research suggests that they can offer a range of benefits for both children and adults.

Every Game has it's benefits

Many games require players to strategize, overcome obstacles, and make decisions to progress, fostering critical thinking and problem-solving abilities and Many find that immersing themselves in games can be a fun and effective way to relax, de-stress, and take a break from daily pressures, Finally Multiplayer and online games can foster communication, teamwork, and collaboration, as players work together to achieve common goals.
